Predicting House Price based on historical data


Project Overview

The price of a house is a complex function of many factors, including the location of the house, the size of the house, the number of bedrooms and bathrooms, the condition of the house, and the current market conditions. Predicting the price of a house is a challenging task, but it is one that is important for both buyers and sellers. Buyers want to know how much they should be willing to pay for a house, and sellers want to know how much they can sell their house for.

In this project, we will build a model to predict the price of a house. We will use a dataset of house sales data to train our model. The dataset includes information on the location of the house, the size of the house, the number of bedrooms and bathrooms, the condition of the house, and the sale price of the house.

We will extract usefull features from input data and then, We will use a variety of machine learning algorithms to train our model. We will evaluate the performance of our models using a held-out dataset. We will then select the best model and use it to make predictions on new data.

The results of this project will be useful for both buyers and sellers. Buyers can use the model to get an estimate of the price of a house before they make an offer. Sellers can use the model to set a realistic asking price for their house.
